Rules and how to play

Welcome to TheoGuessr, the game where you are given a bible verse and you are tasked to figure out where it is. The rules are straightforward: the closer you are to the Bible verse, the more points you earn. you can earn up to 5000 each round, and your top scores will be displayed in the highscores section. Choose a difficulty level, test your knowledge, or play the daily verse and share your score with friends. Remember that you can switch the Bible version at any time. The difficulty slider adjusts the number of context verses: easy includes 6 context verses, medium has 2, and hard provides no context verses. Thank you for playing TheoGuessr, and may you enjoy the journey through the verses!
